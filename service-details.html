Simplifying Regulatory Approvals for Businesses in Nigeria

Qevorix provides end-to-end regulatory registration and compliance support for businesses across industries including food, cosmetics, pharmaceuticals, manufacturing, import/export, and general trade. We help you navigate complex approval processes with ease and professionalism.

  • Fast and professional NAFDAC product registration support.
  • Expert guidance on CAC business registration and incorporation.
  • Assistance with SON certification and compliance requirements.

Our team ensures your documentation is accurate, complete, and aligned with regulatory standards to avoid delays and rejections during processing.

Whether you are launching a new product or expanding your business, Qevorix provides reliable compliance support to help you operate legally and confidently in the Nigerian market.
