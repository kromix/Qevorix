Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our regulatory registration, compliance, and business support services.

What services does Qevorix provide?

Qevorix provides regulatory registration and compliance services including NAFDAC registration, CAC business registration, SON certification, pharmaceutical licensing, import/export documentation, and compliance consulting.

How long does NAFDAC registration take?

The timeline for NAFDAC registration depends on the product category, documentation completeness, and regulatory review process. Our team works to ensure applications are processed efficiently and without delays.

Can Qevorix help startups and small businesses?

Yes. We support startups, SMEs, manufacturers, distributors, importers, and established businesses seeking regulatory approval and compliance assistance in Nigeria.

What documents are required for registration?

Required documents vary depending on the regulatory agency and product category. Our team will guide you through the exact documentation needed for your application.

Do you assist with product labeling and packaging compliance?

Yes. We provide guidance on product labeling, packaging standards, and regulatory compliance requirements to improve approval success.

How can I get started with Qevorix?

You can contact our team through the contact section of the website to schedule a consultation and discuss your registration or compliance requirements.
